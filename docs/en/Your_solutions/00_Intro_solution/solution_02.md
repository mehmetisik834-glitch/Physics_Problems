# Solving Systems of Linear Equations

## 1. Definitions and Formulas

### System of Linear Equations
A **system of linear equations** is a set of two or more linear equations involving the same set of variables. The solution to the system is the set of values for the variables that makes all equations true simultaneously.

### Methods of Solving
1. **Substitution Method:** Solve one equation for one variable and substitute that expression into the other equation.
2. **Elimination Method:** Add or subtract the equations to eliminate one variable, making it possible to solve for the other.

### Standard Form
A linear equation in two variables is typically written as:

$$
ax + by = c
$$

Where $a$, $b$, and $c$ are constants.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Find the values of $x$ and $y$ that satisfy both equations:

1.  $2x + 3y = 12$
2.  $x - y = 1$

---

## 3. Step-by-Step Solution

We will solve this system using the **Substitution Method**.

### Step 1: Solve one equation for one variable
We take the second equation because it is simpler to isolate $x$.

$$
x - y = 1
$$

Add $y$ to both sides:

$$
x = y + 1
$$

### Step 2: Substitute the expression into the other equation
Now, substitute $x = y + 1$ into the first equation: $2x + 3y = 12$.

$$
2(y + 1) + 3y = 12
$$

### Step 3: Solve for the remaining variable ($y$)
Distribute the $2$:

$$
2y + 2 + 3y = 12
$$

Combine the like terms ($2y$ and $3y$):

$$
5y + 2 = 12
$$

Subtract $2$ from both sides:

$$
5y = 10
$$

Divide by $5$:

$$
y = 2
$$

### Step 4: Back-substitute to find the first variable ($x$)
Now that we have the value of $y$, substitute it back into our expression for $x$ from Step 1:

$$
x = y + 1
$$

$$
x = 2 + 1
$$

$$
x = 3
$$

---

## 4. Verification

To ensure the solution is correct, plug $x = 3$ and $y = 2$ into the original equations.

**Equation 1:**

$$
2(3) + 3(2) = 6 + 6 = 12
$$

(True)

**Equation 2:**

$$
3 - 2 = 1
$$

(True)

---

## Final Answer

The solution to the system of equations is:

$$
x = 3, \quad y = 2
$$
